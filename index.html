Lore

Pobblyboo was born from tiny glowing bubbles drifting deep into the sea. Each bubble carried a spark of joy until a pastel kingdom awakened. Now the realm gently restores its Bubblelight through warmth and wonder.

Vision

Become the world’s largest cute and whimsical underwater IP, connecting imagination, stories, and community into one living universe.

Mission

Build a strong IP world through stories, visuals, and character-driven experiences that spread joy and creativity.
